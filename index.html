About Titans East Coast

Titans East Coast Academy (TECA)

Titans East Coast Academy (TECA) is the East Coast branch of Titans Agency headquartered in Seremban. The academy develops young entrepreneurs through structured training in sales, marketing, personal branding, and leadership.

TECA memfokuskan pembangunan usahawan muda di Pantai Timur melalui latihan praktikal dan bimbingan berfasa.

Network Focus / Fokus Rangkaian

  • Kelantan
  • Terengganu
  • Pahang

Mission: To develop independent young entrepreneurs who want to build professional careers in sales and leadership.

Misi: Melahirkan usahawan muda berdikari dengan kerjaya profesional dalam jualan dan kepimpinan.
